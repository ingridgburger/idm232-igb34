Ancho-Orange Chicken

With Kale Rice & Roasted Carrots

Cook time: 45 min | Serves: 4 | Calories: 600

Back to Home

Overview

This dish features chicken glazed with smoky ancho chile paste and orange juice, paired with kale rice and roasted carrots.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 orange (juiced)
  • 1 tbsp ancho chile paste
  • 2 cups kale rice
  • 3 carrots

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and roast carrots until tender.
  2. Cook rice, then stir in kale and raisins.
  3. Mix ancho chile paste and orange juice; glaze the chicken.
  4. Serve chicken with rice and carrots.
