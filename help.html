Help

My Cookbook allows you to browse, search, and view recipes.

Browsing Recipes

  • The homepage displays all available recipes.
  • Each card shows the recipe's basic information at a glance.
  • Select “View Recipe” to see more in depth ingredients, steps, and details.

Searching

Use the search bar to look up recipes by:

  • Titles
  • Ingredients
  • Keywords

Filtering

You may use the filter dropdown to filter recipes by their protein:

  • Beef
  • Chicken
  • Fish
  • Pork
  • Steak
  • Turkey
  • Vegetarian

Recipe Pages

After navigating to a recipe page, you will see:

  • Image of Dish
  • Ingredients
  • Steps
  • Step Images
